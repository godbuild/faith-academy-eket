Welcome to Faith Academy, Eket

At Faith Academy Day Secondary School, Eket, we develop academically sound, spiritually grounded and creatively gifted students. Our commitment is to excellence and character in every student journey.

About Us

Faith Academy was founded to raise a generation of leaders through a balanced approach of education, spirituality and innovation. Located in Eket, Akwa Ibom, we are proud of our excellent track record.

The Principal's Message

In Faith Academy Eket, we believe that every child is a unique gift with immense potential waiting to be unlocked. Our mission is to nurture academic excellence, spirituality, and creativity in each student. We are committed to creating a safe and inspiring learning environment that fosters intellectual curiosity and moral integrity.

Through dedicated teaching staff, modern facilities, and a comprehensive curriculum, we prepare our students not only to excel in exams but also to become responsible and compassionate leaders of tomorrow. We encourage our pupils to explore their talents, embrace challenges, and develop a lifelong love for learning.

As the principal, I am proud of our school’s rich tradition of discipline, respect, and community spirit. Together with parents and guardians, we work hand-in-hand to ensure every student thrives academically and socially. Our students’ achievements in academics, sports, and arts stand as a testament to the hard work and commitment that defines the global trademark of Faith Academy.

We invite you to be part of this vibrant community where values and knowledge go hand in hand. Join us in shaping a brighter future for our children, empowered with the wisdom and courage to make positive impacts in the world.

Mrs. Theresa God'swill
Principal, Faith Academy, Eket.
